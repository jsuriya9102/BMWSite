Awards & Recognition

Our design team always embarks on the design of new models with the highest aspirations. After all, only if something convinces us at BMW, will it fulfil the aspiration of being unique. These results speak for themselves. In the last two years alone, BMW has impressed the juries of countless prominent national and international awards with innovative designs. An achievement that we are proud of – and one which spurs us on to repeatedly create the exceptional.

The German Design Award is the premium prize of the German Design Council, which is one of the world's leading design-competence centres. This prize has been won by BMW several times: in 2012 for the BMW 5 Series Touring, in 2013 for the BMW 6 Series Convertible and the BMW 1 Series (5-door) and in 2014 for the BMW i3.

The iF (Industrie Forum Design e.V.) Design Award is one of the world's leading design competitions. In 2014, this prestigious award was won by the BMW 2 Series Coupé, BMW 3 Series Gran Turismo, BMW 4 Series Coupé and BMW X5. One year earlier, the BMW 3 Series Sedan and BMW 3 Series Touring had scooped the honours. In 2012, it was Gold for the BMW 6 Series Coupé, with the BMW 1 Series, BMW 6 Series Convertible and BMW 1 Series M Coupé also receiving prizes in the same year.

The Red Dot Design Award is one of the largest and most prestigious design competitions in the world. In recent years, BMW has enjoyed many triumphs: In 2014, the BMW 2 Series Coupé, BMW 3 Series Gran Turismo and the new BMW 4 Series models took the honours. In 2013, the BMW 1 Series (3-door), BMW 3 Series Touring and BMW M6 Gran Coupé were among the winners. In 2012, BMW triumphed four times, with prizes for the BMW 6 Series Coupé, BMW 1 Series M Coupé, BMW 6 Series Gran Coupé and BMW M5 Sedan.

The Green Good Design Award forms part of the world's oldest design prize, the international Good Design. This special award focuses on contemporary design and its sustainability credentials. In this way, BMW has been acknowledged several times for its forward-looking car design: In 2010 for the BMW Vision EfficientDynamics and in 2012 for the BMW i3 Concept and the BMW i8 Concept.

The Automotive Brand Contest is an international design competition for automobile brands initiated by the German Design Council. The success of BMW in this competition is more than impressive: in 2011, the winners were SESTOSENSO by Paul Cocksedge, BMW 328 Hommage and BMW 6 Series Coupé. In 2012, the prize went to the BMW i8 Concept Spyder (Design and Innovation of the Year), the BMW Zagato Coupé (image film), the BMW Design Studio Live (Event) and the design of BMW 3 Series Sedan and BMW 6 Series Gran Coupé. Furthermore, the BMW Design team was awarded the title Team of the Year. In 2013, the honours were scooped by the BMW Z4, the BMW 3 Series Gran Turismo, the BMW M6 Gran Coupé and Ronan und Erwan Bouroullec’s QUIET MOTION installation.

BMW Group wins four awards in the J.D. Power APEAL study. The BMW 5 Series range, BMW X3, BMW Z4 and MINI Countryman emerge as winners in the latest customer survey of the North American car market – with current BMW and MINI models scoring top-three placings.

Schomp BMW is awarded a 2022 Dealer of the Year Award in Colorado by DealerRater, a leading car dealer review and reputation management platform and company of Cars.com Inc. (NYSE: CARS). The Dealer of the Year Award recognizes auto dealerships across the U.S. and Canada that are evaluated first-hand by shoppers and report best-in-class in customer service, quality of work, friendliness, pricing and overall experience in their reviews in their state.

The World Car Awards (WCOTY) is an automobile Car of the Year award selected by a jury of 82 international automotive journalists from 24 countries. Cars considered must be sold in at least five countries, on at least two continents prior to 1 January of the year of the award. The contest was inaugurated in 2003, and officially launched in January 2004.For 2006, in addition to the WCOTY award, the performance, green, and design categories were added; the award was announced at the New York International Auto Show. The BMW 3 Series, Mazda MX-5, and Porsche Cayman were the top three finalists.

The BMW M5 (combined fuel consumption: 10.5 l/100km*; combined CO2 emissions: 241 g/100km*) has been voted "World Performance Car 2018", making this the seventh time BMW has received a World Car Award from the organisation "World Car of the Year". The award ceremony was held on 28 March 2018 at the 2018 New York International Auto Show.
